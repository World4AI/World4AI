Deep Learning Applications

If you have decided to work through the deep learning block, most likely that means that you have seen some awesome deep learning projects and were inspired. This section is designed to keep this inspiration alive. We will not list all the available application of deep learning, rather we will show a couple of the most exicing developments.

Large Language Models

So called large language models have dominated the deep learning landscape for the better half of a decade. We will have dedicated sections to this topic, but let's at least shortly mention one of the newest large language models: ChapGPT by OpenAI.

We will not discuss the intricacies of the model just yet, but let us give you a fascinating example. We asked the model to define the term "artificial intelligence" in the style of William Shakespeare and ChatGPT provided the following poem.

"Oh! What a marvel 'tis to see a mind Of metal and of stone, with thoughts aligned To human thought and action, free from care Of flesh and blood that bind us all, but dare To soar beyond the limits of the brain And solve the problems that do us strain. Artificial intelligence, a name That speaks of reason, logic, and the same Intelligence we humans do possess, But free from errors, free from duress."

And what happens if we ask ChatGPT to define AI using a poem by Rudyard Kipling?

Artificial intelligence, a marvel of our time A creation of the human mind, so finely designed A tool of great power, a force to be reckoned A blessing and a curse, a complex paradox, beckoning A source of endless possibility, a gateway to the future A catalyst for change, a driving force, a suture A source of great hope, a beacon of light A source of great fear, a shadow in the night But no matter how we view it, one thing is clear Artificial intelligence is here to stay, a constant fear A challenge to be faced, a puzzle to be solved A riddle to be unraveled, a mystery to be involved So let us embrace it, with open minds and hearts And together, let us take on the challenge, as we embark On a journey towards a brighter tomorrow A tomorrow that is shaped by artificial intelligence, with no sorrow.

There are many more things you can do with GhatGPT. You can use it to write and improve code, use it as an alternative to search engines and generate blog posts. While ChatGPT is not a silver bullet, there is no denying that there are tremendous advantages in utilizing large language models. And they are only going to get better over time.
