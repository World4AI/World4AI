PyTorch Basics

All World4AI examples and exercises are developed with the help of the deep leanring package PyTorch. To bring you up to speed, this chapter is exclusively dedicated to fundamental PyTorch knowledge. We are going to cover the fundamental Tensor class, the autograd package, the Dataset and DataLoader classes. At the end of this chapter we will implement a full training loop and try to classify handwritten digits.
